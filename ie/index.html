Optum Internship

300+ Hour Experience

Learn More

In the summer of 2022, I was a Technology Development Program intern at Optum, a subsidiary of UnitedHealth Group. I was part of the behavioral health division and was in a group of 5 interns. We were tasked with identifying a healthcare need and developing a minimum viable product (MVP) addressing it.

We learned that the national average wait time for behavioral health services is 48 days. We leveraged journaling as a tool to continuously engage clients during wait times and improve treatment outcomes. Our MVP was well-received by Optum management. For me, it was a challenging yet rewarding dive into the world of innovation and entrepreneurship.
